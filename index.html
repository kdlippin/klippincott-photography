About

Keith literally means "From the Forest". Isn't that funny? After about 25 years of carrying the name I realized it fit perfectly. I really like it because, it emphasizes that there is a forest out there much bigger than me. So this whole website of "forest" is really not actually "Keith", it's where "Keith" comes from. It is the places I've gone, the stories I've heard, and the things I have thought about. The reality is, we are all a product of our experience and often the experience we're handed is not chosen. It's a gift. And it is a gift I am giving back through this site. Enjoy!
